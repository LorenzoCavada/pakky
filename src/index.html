What is Pakky?

Pakky is the first mailbox with a keyless opening that, even in case of your absence, can still receive letters and packages of medium/small dimension. It can also be used for exchanging goods without the need of any actual contacts. Different from other already existing mailbox, Pakky can be opened by simply using a QR code available throw our app. Receiving packages has never been so easy!

Features

Easy

Receiveing new parcels and letters has never been so easy. The courier will arrive in front of pakky and will scan the barcode with the tracking code using the apposite pakky sensor. Than pakky will open itself allowing the courier to deposit safely the package and, by closing again the device, s/he and any other people won't be able to open again Pakky.

Safe

Thanks to our innovative design and concept Pakky is completly waterproof and can resist to any time of natural events. Its strong locking system will also prevent any attempt to force it. The use of a special letter entry will also prevent anyone to insert garbage inside yur Pakky.

Smart

Pakky comes with a ton of features all customizable and confortably available from our unique app. Pakky will send you a notification whenever a new letter/packages will be inserted so you will never miss an important delivery!
